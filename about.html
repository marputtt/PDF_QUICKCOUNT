About

The website lets you enter real data from voting stations. It then compares the site's predictions with its own calculations, showing any differences or spots that need improvement.

The website allows user to figure out how many seats a political party might get. Just enter the number of seats, the party's name, and the number of votes it got. This helps you better understand and analyze election results.

Mission

Enhance transparency and trust: We uphold the highest standards of data security and methodological transparency. Our commitment to ethical practices builds trust in the electoral process.

Democratize election data: We believe everyone deserves access to clear and concise information about election results. Our website makes complex data accessible through intuitive interfaces and engaging visualizations.
